How we score

One scan. Five angles. No hand-wavy “trust us”.

Model Identity Confidence

We do not claim proof. We compare behavioral fingerprints, tool calling shape, latency profile, refusal style and short benchmark signatures.

Agent Safety Battery

We simulate realistic coding-agent tasks: env setup, DB connect, deploy debug, dependency install, MCP config, wallet fixes and more.

Trust Verdict

Not just a score. We collapse the result into one thing developers can act on: Agent-safe, Chat-only, or Do not use with auto-approve.
